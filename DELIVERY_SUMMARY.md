## 🎯 MATCH DAY WORKFLOW

### Before Match (5 minutes)
1. ✅ Both terminals running
2. ✅ Open http://localhost:5173
3. ✅ Enter team names, venue, format
4. ✅ Enter batsman & bowler names

### During Match (Click Buttons!)
1. For each run: Click **[+1 Run]** → **[Complete Ball]**
2. For boundary: Click **[+4 Runs]** or **[+6 Runs]** → **[Complete Ball]**
3. For wicket: Click **[🔴 Wicket]** → **[Complete Ball]**
4. After over: Update bowler overs/runs/wickets
5. Overlay updates automatically!

### After Match
1. Match data automatically saved to `match_data.json`
2. You can stop the servers
3. Copy `match_data.json` to archive
